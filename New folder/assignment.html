Alex Wilson

I often find it hard to express myself through words and art gives me the freedom to communicate what I can’t say effectively. When I feel blue, I can make something that represents that; or I can create something beautiful that can distract me from the unpleasantness of my day. Whether it’s photography, painting or building a fence; I find them all hands on outlets for my creativity.
